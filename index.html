Bible Translation Begins With Understanding

A simple framework for understanding Bible translation.

Start Learning Explore the Framework

Every faithful Bible translation process is built on three foundational principles.

Understand

Learn the meaning of Scripture — what the text meant to its original audience.

Translate

Communicate the meaning in another language — clearly, naturally, and faithfully.

Check

Confirm faithfulness and approve language use so the community trusts the translation.

The Six Activities of the Framework

Understand

  • Share Scripture
  • Learn Meaning

Translate

  • Communicate Meaning
  • Record Translation

Check

  • Confirm Faithfulness
  • Approve Language Use
